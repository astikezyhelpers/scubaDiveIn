Cancellations and Refunds Policy

Last updated: {{ current_date }}

1. Course Cancellations

For diving courses:

  • Cancellation 7+ days before course: 90% refund
  • Cancellation 3-7 days before course: 50% refund
  • Cancellation less than 3 days: No refund
  • Medical emergencies: Case-by-case consideration

2. Equipment Returns

For diving equipment purchases:

  • Unused items: 14-day return window
  • Original packaging required
  • Shipping costs for returns are customer's responsibility
  • Custom or personalized items are non-refundable

3. Refund Process

Refunds will be processed:

  • Within 7-10 business days
  • To the original payment method
  • After inspection of returned items
  • Minus any applicable fees

4. Weather Cancellations

For weather-related cancellations:

  • Full refund or rescheduling option
  • Based on local weather authority advisories
  • Safety is our primary concern
  • Alternative dates will be offered

5. Special Circumstances

We consider exceptions for:

  • Medical emergencies with documentation
  • Natural disasters
  • Government-imposed restrictions
  • Technical issues with our services

6. Processing Fees

The following fees may apply:

  • Administrative fee: 5% of refund amount
  • Payment processing fees
  • Shipping and handling costs
  • Restocking fees for equipment
