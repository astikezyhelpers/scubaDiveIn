Course Overview

The Open Water Diver course is your first step into the underwater world. This comprehensive program combines classroom learning, pool training, and open water dives to give you the skills and knowledge needed to become a certified scuba diver.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic scuba diving equipment and its use
  • Essential diving skills and techniques
  • Safety procedures and emergency protocols
  • Underwater communication
  • Dive planning and execution
  • Environmental awareness and conservation

Course Structure

Classroom Sessions

5 theory sessions covering diving fundamentals, equipment, and safety procedures.

Pool Training

5 confined water sessions to practice and perfect your diving skills.

Open Water Dives

4 open water dives to apply your skills in real conditions.
